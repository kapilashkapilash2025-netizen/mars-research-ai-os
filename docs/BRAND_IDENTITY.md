# Areograph Labs — Working Brand Identity

> Working identity only. Complete trademark, company-name, domain, and jurisdiction-specific legal
> clearance before commercial use.

## Core idea

**Areograph Labs** builds verifiable intelligence for planetary research and autonomous exploration.
The name comes from *areography*: the mapping and scientific description of Mars.

## Mission

**Make planetary intelligence verifiable.**

We turn planetary evidence, simulation, and autonomous-system decisions into knowledge that scientists,
engineers, students, and mission teams can inspect, reproduce, and trust.

## Brand architecture

- **Company:** Areograph Labs
- **Platform:** Mars Research AI OS
- **First public product:** Mars Knowledge Console
- **Future flagship:** Mars Mission Control Simulator

## Message system

- Primary line: **Make planetary intelligence verifiable.**
- Product line: **Ask Mars. Trace every answer.**
- Engineering principle: **Evidence first. Inference labeled. Decisions replayable.**
- Community invitation: **Build the intelligence layer for the next frontier.**

## Personality

- Ambitious, never exaggerated
- Scientific, never sterile
- Futuristic, never fictional
- Open, inspectable, and evidence-led
- Calm under mission-critical conditions

## Visual system

- Void black represents the unknown.
- Mars rust represents the physical world and field evidence.
- Signal lime represents verified machine state.
- Spectral cyan represents traceable information flow.
- The orbital `A` mark combines a planetary horizon, trajectory, and upward vector.

Use compact monospace labels for system state, strong condensed display typography for mission messages,
and generous negative space around the company mark. Motion should suggest scanning, orbit, and signal
acquisition and must respect reduced-motion preferences.

## Language guardrails

Do not claim NASA affiliation, flight readiness, calibrated hardware performance, discovered science, or
autonomous safety certification. Clearly identify simulations, candidate predictions, assumptions, and
future capabilities. Credibility is the brand advantage.
